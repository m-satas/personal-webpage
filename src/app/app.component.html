About

My fascination with technology began in early childhood, sparked by my love for computer games and interactive experiences. Introduced to Pascal by my father, I was captivated by the potential of coding to bring ideas to life.

Coding, for me, is akin to solving puzzles, even more than that - it's about figuring out the puzzle yourself and making algorithms to teach the computer to solve the puzzle on your behalf.

As I grew, I engaged in different creative mediums, from exploring animation in Flash to experimenting with Photoshop, where I discovered my interest for digital design.

These experiences led me to pursue programming and visual design further in university, where I chose a program that offered modules in creating visuals and programming to create interactive experiences.

Now I continue to explore coding and visual design, driven by the joy of creation and the lessons learned from the process.
